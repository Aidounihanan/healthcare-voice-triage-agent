# Simplified triage guidelines (for demo )

- Sudden chest pain, shortness of breath, loss of consciousness:
  => Critical emergency. Recommend immediate emergency department visit.

- High fever, cough, flu-like symptoms for less than 3 days:
  => Moderate urgency. Recommend appointment within 24-48 hours.

- Mild symptoms, no major risk factors:
  => Low urgency. Recommend appointment within 3-5 days.

- Pregnancy with strong abdominal pain:
  => High urgency. Recommend urgent evaluation.
